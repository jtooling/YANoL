YANoL

About

YANoL (**Yet Another Notification Library**) is an extensible framework aiming at providing a complete and easy to use engine in order to manage and broadcast messages through your applications.

Authors

YANoL is maintained by Antoine Neveux , Paulo Roberto Massa Cereda and Herbert Veitengruber .

Documentation

All of our documentation should be found either in the javadoc, but also on our website, or finally on our wiki.
